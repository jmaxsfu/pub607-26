# This is my life story

It was a dark and stormy night. The wind howled. Rain lashed at the windows. The dog was silent. The cat was too.

Suddently, there was a knock at the window. My mother leapt up and opened the window. There was a bird, a *stork*, carrying a bundle. My mother took the bundle from the bird.

It was a baby. It was me.
