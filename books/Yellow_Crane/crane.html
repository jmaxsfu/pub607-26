万古烧

我买了一口好锅
可以用一辈子的那种
陶土的，有松木盖的
只要天塌不下来
我就可以一直用它
煲汤，烧肉
但更多的时候我宁愿
它就那样闲置着
像我一样空空如也
却不可测度

THE EVERLASTING POT

I bought a good pot
one that can last a whole life
made of clay, with a pinewood lid
As long as the sky does not collapse
I can use it all the time
to cook soup, stew meat
But more often I’d rather
it just lay idle there
like me, empty
yet unfathomable
